Memberships

1. Non-Profit Membership

Cost: $250 per year

Benefits:

  • Listing in the chamber's online directory
  • Access to chamber networking events
  • Subscription to chamber newsletters
  • Participation in chamber committees
  • Recognition as a non-profit supporter on the chamber's website

2. Bronze Membership

Cost: $500 per year

Benefits (includes Non-Profit benefits plus):

  • Enhanced listing in the chamber's online directory with a logo and description
  • Discounted rates for chamber-sponsored training sessions and workshops
  • Access to member-only networking events
  • One free spotlight feature on the chamber's website homepage per year
  • Access to member-to-member discount programs
  • Opportunity to host a chamber networking event at their location

3. Silver Membership

Cost: $1,000 per year

Benefits (includes Bronze benefits plus):

  • Priority placement in the chamber's online directory
  • Two free spotlight features on the chamber's website homepage per year
  • Exclusive invitation to a quarterly executive luncheon with local business leaders
  • Sponsorship opportunities for chamber events and programs
  • Additional discounts on advertising opportunities with the chamber
  • Access to member-only educational webinars and seminars

4. Gold Membership

Cost: $2,500 per year

Benefits (includes Silver benefits plus):

  • Premier placement and enhanced listing in the chamber's online directory
  • Four free spotlight features on the chamber's website homepage per year
  • Recognition as a Gold Member in chamber communications and events
  • Complimentary tickets to all chamber networking events and workshops
  • Priority sponsorship opportunities at major chamber events
  • Annual private consultation with chamber leadership on business issues
  • Special invitation to an exclusive annual appreciation dinner with the chamber board
